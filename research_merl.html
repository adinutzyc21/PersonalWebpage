Research > MERL Internship

Spatial Analysis Group, Summer 2014

In the summer of 2014, I interned at Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories in Boston, where I worked in the Spatial Analysis group with Dr. Srikumar Ramalingam and Dr. Yuichi Taguchi.

Because I signed a Non-Disclosure Agreement with MERL before joining the project, I cannot show code or explain the approach we used. However, I can tell you that the aim of the project was to create a better 3D reconstruction algorithm by using both images and 3D models.
